Body Types & Styling Guide

Understanding your body type helps you choose outfits that enhance your natural shape. No matter what, remember— Every Form is Beautiful!

Women’s Body Types

Apple Shape

Broader upper body, slimmer legs. Flowy dresses and V-necklines work best!

Pear Shape

Narrow upper body, wider hips. A-line skirts and off-shoulder tops create balance.

Hourglass

Defined waist with balanced upper and lower body. Fitted clothing enhances curves.

Rectangle

Evenly distributed proportions. Belts and structured outfits add definition.

Men’s Body Types

Triangle

Wider waist, narrower shoulders. Layered outfits add structure.

Rectangle

Shoulders and waist are aligned. Blazers and fitted shirts add shape.

Inverted Triangle

Broad shoulders, narrow waist. Slim-fit clothing enhances proportions.

Oval

Fuller midsection. Dark colors and vertical patterns create balance.

💖 Every Form is Beautiful 💖

No matter your shape, confidence is the best outfit you can wear!
